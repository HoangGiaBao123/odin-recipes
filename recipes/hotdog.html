Hotdog

Ingredients

  • 1 Hotdog bun
  • 1 Beef frankfurter (sausage)
  • 1 tablespoon Mustard or Ketchup
  • 1 tablespoon Relish or chopped onions (optional)

Steps

  1. Boil, grill, or pan-fry the sausage for about 5 minutes until heated through.
  2. Lightly steam or toast the hotdog bun.
  3. Place the cooked sausage inside the bun.
  4. Add mustard, ketchup, and your favorite toppings on top.
  5. Serve immediately while hot.
