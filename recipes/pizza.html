Pizza

Ingredients

  • 1 Pizza dough ball
  • 1/2 cup Pizza sauce
  • 1 1/2 cups Shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 20-25 slices of Pepperoni
  • 1 tablespoon Olive oil

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface into a circle.
  3. Spread the pizza sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a small border for the crust.
  4. Sprinkle the mozzarella cheese generously over the sauce.
  5. Place the pepperoni slices on top of the cheese.
  6. Bake for 12-15 minutes until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbling.
