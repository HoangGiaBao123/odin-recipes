Mochi

Ingredients

  • 100g Glutinous rice flour (Shiratamako)
  • 2 tablespoons Sugar
  • 150ml Water
  • Cornstarch (for dusting)
  • 3 Fresh strawberries

Steps

  1. Mix glutinous rice flour, sugar, and water in a microwave-safe bowl.
  2. Cover with plastic wrap and microwave for 2 minutes. Stir well until sticky.
  3. Dust your work surface with plenty of cornstarch.
  4. Flatten the warm dough and cut it into small circles.
  5. Wrap each piece of dough around a strawberry and seal the edges.
  6. Let it cool down before serving.
